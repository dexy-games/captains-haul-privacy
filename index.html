Privacy Policy for Captain's Haul

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

Dexy Games ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Captain's Haul mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Game Data

  • High Scores and Gameplay Data: We collect your high scores, game statistics, and gameplay achievements to provide leaderboard functionality through PlayFab.
  • Player Name: An automatically generated player name (e.g., "Captain1234") is created for leaderboard display.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for game performance and compatibility.

1.2 Third-Party Services

Our game uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Unity Ads: Displays advertisements and collects advertising identifiers. See Unity Privacy Policy
  • PlayFab (Microsoft): Provides leaderboard and game backend services. See Microsoft Privacy Statement

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display your scores on global and friend leaderboards
  • Track your in-game achievements and progress
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through Unity Ads
  • Provide in-app purchase functionality (Remove Ads)

3. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored securely on PlayFab's servers (Microsoft Azure). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Children's Privacy

Captain's Haul is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game uses anonymous player identifiers and does not require account creation.

5. Your Rights

  • Data Deletion: You can reset your game data by uninstalling the app. This will remove all local data.
  • Leaderboard Data: Leaderboard entries are anonymous and cannot be directly linked to you.
  • Opt-Out of Ads: You can purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase to stop seeing advertisements.

6. Advertising

We display advertisements through Unity Ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

7. In-App Purchases

We offer a "Remove Ads" purchase. Purchases are handled by the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). We do not store your payment information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

9. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using Captain's Haul, you consent to this transfer.
